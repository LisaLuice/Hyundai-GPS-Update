Hyundai GPS Update

Update

Why You Need to Update

Keeping your Hyundai GPS up-to-date ensures accurate directions, safer travel, and access to the latest road information. Regular updates improve functionality and reduce navigation errors.

How to Check Your System

To determine if an update is available, go to your Hyundai vehicle’s infotainment system, navigate to Settings > System Info, and check the current software version.

Step-by-Step Update Guide

Visit the official Hyundai update site or trusted providers like Navisolve. Download the update software to a USB, insert it into your vehicle, and follow the on-screen instructions.

Benefits of Updating

Updated GPS maps include new roads, POIs (Points of Interest), improved voice navigation, and faster route calculations — ensuring a smooth driving experience every time.

Troubleshooting Tips

If the update fails, ensure the USB is formatted correctly (FAT32), has sufficient storage, and is not interrupted during the process. You can also contact support for assistance.
